Strategic Partnership in AMU
NOVA ENGINEERING is the exclusive agent for De Lama in the following countries of Arab Maghreb Union:
  • Morocco
  • Tunisia
  • Libya

As the exclusive agent, NOVA ENGINEERING provides sales, installation and maintenance services for De Lama's products in these markets.
De Lama is a leading manufacturer of sterilization and decontamination equipment for the pharmaceutical, biotechnology and medical industries.
NOVA ENGINEERING and De Lama share a common vision of delivering high-quality solutions that meet the needs and expectations of their customers.
